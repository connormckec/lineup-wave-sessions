⚙️ Watcher settings

These are Railway env vars you can set in the Variables tab to tune the watcher.

NTFY_TOPIC — your push notification topic
LOW_SLOTS_THRESHOLD — alert when slots ≤ this (default: 2)
CHECK_EVERY_MINS — how often to check (default: 5)
SMS_ADDRESS — your carrier SMS email address
